# Aage Badhun

Ye har ek dafa main
Dhundhta Teri palken
Wo chuen ek dusre ko to thoda
Aage badhun
Tere hoton tak pahunchna na hai
Mujhko
Teri Aankhen mujhko rokti hain
Meri hoton tak pahunchna hai tujhko
Teri batain tujhko rokti hain
ye har ek dafa main dhundhta
Teri aankon ko wo mile na mujhse
To main thoda aage badhun

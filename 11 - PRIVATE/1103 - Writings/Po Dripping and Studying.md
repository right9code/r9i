# Dripping and Studying


Raining ⛈️ like wicked
sounds like cricket
As I walk through roads
filled with holes
Drives passing by in pride
Making arcs of water
Draining my side
Guess you easily mistake 
my sweat for water right
But here I'm in front of a textbook 
Dripping and studying

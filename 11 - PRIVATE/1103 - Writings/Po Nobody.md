# Nobody Stays

I cannot cry
Don't know why
Everyone i know
go go go away
And why
Nobody stays,
Everyone walks over
Nobody waits
I could just stare sober
Nobody cares

I cannot cry
Don't know why
I have heard it
So many times
Maybe it wasn't
meant to be
Maybe I'm bad
unnotice me
Everyone I show
the dark in glow
stark in snow
grow cold and go
Nobody stays.

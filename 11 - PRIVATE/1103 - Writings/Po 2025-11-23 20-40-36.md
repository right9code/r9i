# (Untitled Poem)

Now I yearn for company
the need for a human touch
Desires for skins to brush 
Ask for. Is it too much?

Tongue is sour no taste
Young but I'm too late
All I can do is long
Now it feels so wrong

Metal should turn melody
The rise and falls a symphony
Guilt of craving and lust
Sorrow.Well it hurts.

Now I burn for company
the need for a human touch
And I could not ask any
May be it is quite too much

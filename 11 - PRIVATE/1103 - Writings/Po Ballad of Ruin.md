# Ballad of Ruin

Why I
end up
in a
sad mood
when I
wake up
to 
not you
But it's true 
I'm 
here
just not you

Why I 
end up
in a
dream I
wake up
and I see
you
But it's true
I'm here
just with you

In the sorrow 
that's tomorrow 
when I see yester better
with ya
All dove down to rest
I was with you

Why I
end up 
in present 
when the gift is gone
I'm fed up
and I wish I knew
It was not suppose to
just me
not you

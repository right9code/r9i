# Like a Bad Habit

Comes a rain pouring down
Times me find me with me frown
few years down all around 
only I found 
like a Bad Habit
In my memory I'm So Sorry
It won't happen again




One Wreck Show
I'm in control 




More like you 
less like me
shadows of yesterday 
on tommorrow street
walking fuzzy
my head ain't straight 
too much commotion 
on the memory lane




Ta

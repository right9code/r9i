# Kya Likhte Syah


Ye likhte likhte likhte syah
Aankken likhte likhte kya
baaten likhte uljhi lat ki
fir suljhe man ko likhte kya
Naapen unko chum kar jo
Baatein unko puch kar jo^
fir hoth see le honton se
fir khamoshi likhte kya
likhte kya saanson ka badhna
jhuki nazar ko dhundte Syah
fir uthi nazar to rukti saans
aur mohabbat pe bikte Syah
likhte likhte likhte kya

ye ruh chum kar aa jate
ya aanken mund sharma jaate
ya hoti baatein nayi nayi
ya beete kal samaa jaate
ye dard bada marham hota
pehla bhada madham hota
hum pyas namak kar jee jate
phir sehed samjh kar pee jate
aur bhar jati wo tanhayi sari
ked ungliyaan main ungliyaan karte
agar mohabbat pe bikte Syah
to likhte likhte likhte kya

# (Untitled Poem)

The rotors in the sky/
Spinning like my head/
When I think of you/
And the thoughts that fly/

Remember Sundays,Why?/
Remember other days, I/
use to spend with you around/
Until You were lost not to be found/

Clouds are our memories
It keeps changing
Every time I see it fade in
From vapour to moisture

From moisture to dreams

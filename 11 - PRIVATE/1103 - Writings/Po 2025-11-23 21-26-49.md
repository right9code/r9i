# (Untitled Poem)

Badalte mausam
Basant hemant hote dekha
Sard akele
Maine bahut badalte dekha

Saans se tees
Tees se takaluf
tabiyat badalte dekha

Mar jaane wale
bager mere
unko mere bina
muskurate dekha

Kon kiska syah
Sab kahani hai
kuch vehem paal ke bethe the
aankhen band fir bhi mili jab chasma utar ke beteh the

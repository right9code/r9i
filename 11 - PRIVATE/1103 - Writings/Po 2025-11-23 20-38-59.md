# (Untitled Poem)

water drips down the cheeks
it's after long
I saw it again
like your favourite high school song

the one you cannot have anymore
could have beens?
where eyes could not meet eyes
but you touch without touching

Water drips down 
And reaches the valley
from cheeks to chin
driving slowly

it spreads to find invisibility
cause you could not keep it forever
like your favourite high school song

the one you cannot have anymore...

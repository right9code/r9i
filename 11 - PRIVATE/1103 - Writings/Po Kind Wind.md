# Kind Wind

ye sukriya hawa
ek pal main nam jo hua
chupa bethi
sukha bethi

Is the wind kind ?
when it dries 
the moisture off my eyes
while in the gaze I find
none worth tearing
the paper I'm writing
i'm wearing

every moment
i'm wearing a sheet of 
thin glass 
i blow breath on
let it settle
I write on
maybe just maybe
in the words
I might see
someone who could read 

someone who could read 
how opposites meet
in letters in words 
in laughs in grief
someone who could read
why there's plural of first three
why the grief is alone
even when it's more
why solitude prolongs


a life doesn't find a reader
So I breath her 
what i write i feed her
i breathe more
and vanish and puff 
it's gone
the letters were doors
only windows they see
through to a smile 
and no weep

The wind is kind
as it dries moisture off my eyes.

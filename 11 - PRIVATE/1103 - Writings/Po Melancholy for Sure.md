# Melancholy for Sure

I could write one more
Melancholy for sure
To the glance that change with eyes
and the invitation that changes the door 
From love , like then distant
Clouds that disappeared with rain once more.

R with one less stroke in Rain
Foolish what we hope in Pain


> *By **[[iink]]***

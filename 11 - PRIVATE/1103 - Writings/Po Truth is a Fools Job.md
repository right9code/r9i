# Truth is a Fool's Job

I drink from the heaven
The lies
that suffice
the joker, the clown
or smart in more tries

I cannot be smart
not a possibility 
but I try to be
Realise to be
Real lies to be
Real eyes to be
Or not to be

Truth is a fool's job
the one that I hold
with scars in bold
with my back on the wall
with shards and in shreds 
with blindfolds and in red

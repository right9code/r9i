# (Untitled Poem)


Main badal nahi pa Raha 
Khud ko syah
Meri kosis hai mujh ko
Bhula dene ki
Jazbaat,takaluff, umeedon
Se upar
Khud se alag 
Ho jane ki
Main sota hun
Sirhaane badal badal kar karwat
Rukhsat mujhse mere
Halat nahi hote
Meri kosis hai mujhko 
Bhula dene ki
Par naye fasane yaad 
Nahi hote


Meri kosis hai 
mujko bhula dene ki
Par ek mujh ko 
Main syah 
Bhula nahi pata
Ye umeed takluff
yadein aur kisse
Itar inse har kosis 
Bas ho nahi pata
Tafseel se zayaza/shinakhatt
Karne par 
Haqiqat zindagi
Itni hai
Mayus hun duniya se
Beintehaan magar
Beintehaan magar
Par ro nahi pata

# (Untitled Poem)

Kitne khayal ho skte hain syah
Kitne sawal ho skte hain
Kitne aur khwahishon main dafn
Kitne malal ho sakte hain

Main manzil ko sochta
Sahil ko sochta
Main sochta saare sawal
Fir kitne jawaab ho skte hain

Kitni mushkilen bahar
Kitni Maine bana di
Jehen main bante masale
Behissab ho sakte hain

Mujh se koi azaad 
Kar de mujhko
Kuch mansoobe mere bhi
Kamyaab ho skte hain

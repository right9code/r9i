# Hate


Hate is a helpless hurt
An emotion that drives angst
You breathe shallow
You feel mallow
It's fire that only burns
inside out
I hate a lot
A lot more than most can imagine
Enough to burn the world
But I can't
I'm helpless
So I hate a lot more
and feel hurt more
I hate with the core of my heart
and it will never go away
until world burns and 
I with it.
Hate is a strong emotion
Hate is the strongest emotion.

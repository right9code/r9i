# Sky

my blanket forever
with sparks and ovals
i watch and surrender
to dreams in my mental
Bright i seek you
Dark you seek me.

Innocence washing away
reason comes to shine
I still miss the spots 
they were like moon shine
I was happy and careless 
Then care less and more
i grew up a statement
I'll still write more.

The walls ,the desks, the boards
And hearts
I grew up to that longing
Of friends and start
it's love and emotions
all over again
Maybe it's happy
Maybe it's pain.

# Here This Story Of Mine


I could wrap a blanket around me
Afraid and lonely they said they found me
They offered love with a drop poison 
I drowned in.

Breathless I'm a mess making a fuss
Paper mountain drawing fountains
but I'm thirsty to trust
I frowned in.

Fallin is crazy in love in faith
but slide under feet your fate
Lies and lies me and not me
Where you at 
Smile but already Dead.

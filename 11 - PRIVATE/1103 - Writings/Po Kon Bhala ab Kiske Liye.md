# Kon Bhala ab Kiske Liye

Main karun kya syah
Ye etbaar bacha kiske liye
Ye badalta zamana
Hum musfir
Ye ulazhana fir kiske liye

Main theek tha
Tab gum main duba
Yun hosh main aana
Ab kiske liye

Kiska sukoon
Kisko junoon
Zamana Zali hai
Ye ulazhana fir kiske liye

Ye koi hota jo batata
Ki zindagi sabr ki
Ki itna dard hota hai
Main dub jaata
Jahalat main
Ye sadgi 
Ab kiske liye

Mujhe lauta de koi
Dus saal mere
Warna ye rona
Par ye rona ab
Kiske liye

Duniya dari se sharminda
Kya behtar main zinda
Kya ho ab mera
Tadap saheel par
Ye manzil ab kiske liye

Tum ho kahin
Koi aks ho ya hawa ho
Aur nahi to ban jao
Wehem mera
Ye asal ye haqiqat
Ye mujhko nahi chahiye
Mujhe mili to bata
Kiske liye

Ye main kitni bar
Kitni baar main
Sarkta pahunch shuruvat pr
par har baar ye shuruvat
Bata to zara kiske liye

Ye tadap jo uthati 
Main hun sharminda 
Ye kiski raza ye kiske liye
Kon kahan jo chahe 
Sukoon mera, Kise parwaah 
kon kahaan kiske liye

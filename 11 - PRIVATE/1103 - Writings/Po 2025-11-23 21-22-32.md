# (Untitled Poem)

If you have to think about yourself with me it's not worth it
If I have to think about me with you it's not worth it

Worry about yourself with me it's not worth it
Worry about me with you it's not worth it
If you have to care,If I have to care,for our own
it's not worth it
it's not worth it if our tears are lonely,
If you have to think about yourself with me
it's not worth it
If I have to think about me with you 
it's not worth it


if our tears are solo,
our fears are lonely,
we have to think for our own
it's not worth it

worry and cares
dreams, nightmares 
to go through it alone
it's not worth it

it's not worth it
if you're on your own
I'm on mine
thinking ourselves
all the time

If this is
what it's all about
it's not worth it.

# Unnecessary Amplification of Pain

I live in the overview
In the sky; up and high
In my head
I don't know why
I try
To connect
Resist
to correct
I'm so stupid

I create my own suffering
from the pain
that reach 
to panic for no reason
but to vain it
I'm addict

So stupid
Not to realise
That in my head
are all my vice
misjudgement on the roll
I paid for it all
In losses and more
drowning on shore
Who drowns on shore?
I'm stupid


With losing people
and gaining stains
of corrupt thought
always insane
I wanna be different
yet I'm the same
a wannabe whatever
that's my shame 

I miss a lot
what I can have
but when I have
I throw it back
Into seperation
I live in imagination

People talk to me
I talk to myself
Constantly judging
All my worth
I'm insecure
I wanna be right
now and forever
in every sight
I'm so stupid

I kissed a girl 
I could never feel
I was still in my head
writing a film
I wish I was myopic
I could see and exist
not real
I'm just a wish
I'm stupid

I close my eyes 
what I have
I don't see
I'm stuck in a loop
Of what could have been
I'm resentful and worried
I surrender I panic



I'm so full of me 
and my anxiety
addict to worrying
very selectively



to get out of my head 
Into the life
what will be suffice

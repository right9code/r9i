# (Untitled Poem)

If only one could touch it,
Again and Again
Pearl by pearl
Weave it into a string
The promise into a ring 
And the past that it bring
Making sense as it seems
Never to be lost 
So close no room for NOs
Piece by piece
Limb by limb
Cell by cell
No seperation just dwell
No I 
No You
Just Us.

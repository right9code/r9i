# (Untitled Poem)

solace that it carries to try your best and fail
To balance best to your capacity
Even if it derails
To be one less than needed to be
The blunder that's there
Visible as everything in stark contrast
How can you not see
What's meant to be seen
Pile of failures
Sides to me
Success is such a blanket warm
That finds any skin
Any but not my own
I could not find surrender
But did not find the best
The curtains that fold
Visible
More mess

What goes around and comes back
Every-time I chin up
I fall back
Solace that it carries to burn you all
I could not do that 
So i could not afford.

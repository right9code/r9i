# Guilty

past few months have been very insightful in people, priorities, flaws and things
to know whose excited when the phone rings
see yourself as a attention seeking whore
staying away from difficult decision made for

guilty as charged for lust and desperation 
no people around me maybe that's disfunction 
on account of laziness I've been impressed 
there's a time to get over this deep rest

i've been chasing mirages all my life
my choice people have never been wise
maybe i also have touched being immoral being
stationed in nostalgia when everyone's moving 

rest assured I've been learning my lessons late
maybe it's just me I feel betrayed 
my delusion still hold to punish my pride
this fight I'm alone none by my side


gathered to be great when I don't stand good
overreached and failed and misunderstood 
no real justice to the time that i owned 
i can't complain even if I'm wronged 

i was too late in making right mistakes 
restentful, envious, most of the days
it's gonna be more pain as i move forward 
far from dawn hope still far from the dusk

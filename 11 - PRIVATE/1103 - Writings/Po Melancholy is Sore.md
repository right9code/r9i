# Melancholy is Sore

I could write
One more 
Melancholy is sore
And surrender is patient
I don't have it in me any more
A song of tired 
In clutches of it
It's taking me over
I might fall for it
Why even try
Just to fail
A glimmer is enough
But non one sent that
Who decides what is futile
And turn it dust
Thoughts things desires
Expectations and much

Why don't you deliver
Why you ask for pray

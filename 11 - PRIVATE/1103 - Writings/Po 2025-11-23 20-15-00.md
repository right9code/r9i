# (Untitled Poem)

tu keetab hoja
mere sawal mere sawal
tu jawab hoja
ye tujhko dekhkar
jo kho jata hun main
mujhe dundh andhere main
aftaab ho ja
tu kitaab hoja
to hoja dhup sard
tu os hoja 
main ho jaun pyasa raat bhar
tu ahat ek hisaab hoja 
tu kitaab hoja

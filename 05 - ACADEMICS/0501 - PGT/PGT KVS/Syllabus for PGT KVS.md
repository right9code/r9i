## 📖 Section A: Reading

**Comprehension**
* **Source Material:** Three or four unseen passages.
* **Genres:** Prose, poetry, drama, articles, editorials, scientific, and literary extracts.
* **Skills Tested:**
    * Comprehension & Inference
    * Vocabulary & Tone
    * Rhetorical Devices
    * Logical Sequencing

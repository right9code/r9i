# The Last Leaf
**Author:** O. Henry

## 🎯 Central Idea / Theme
*   **Hope & Willpower:** The will to live can cure illness.
*   **Sacrifice:** Behrman sacrifices his life to save Johnsy. A masterpiece is not just art, but something that saves a life.

## 👥 Key Characters
1.  **Johnsy:** A young artist suffering from pneumonia. She is pessimistic and believes she will die when the last leaf falls.
2.  **Sue:** Johnsy's loyal friend and caretaker.
3.  **Behrman:** An old painter (60 years old). A failure in art but dreams of painting a "masterpiece."

## 📝 Quick Summary (Bullet Points)
*   **The Illness:** Johnsy has pneumonia. The doctor says she has a slim chance because she has lost the will to live.
*   **The Obsession:** Johnsy watches an ivy creeper outside her window. She counts the falling leaves backward (12, 11, 10...). She believes that when the last leaf falls, she will die.
*   **Sue's Worry:** Sue tries to cheer her up but fails. She tells Behrman about Johnsy's fancy.
*   **The Storm:** A heavy storm rages at night. Johnsy is sure the last leaf will fall.
*   **The Miracle:** Next morning, the last leaf is still there! It survives another stormy night.
*   **The Realization:** Johnsy realizes it is a sin to want to die. She decides to live.
*   **The Twist:** The leaf never fell because it was **painted** on the wall by Behrman that stormy night.
*   **The Sacrifice:** Behrman caught pneumonia while painting in the rain and died two days later.
*   **The Masterpiece:** The painted leaf was his masterpiece because it saved a life.

## 🔑 Important Quotes/Expressions
*   *"She has made up her mind that she’s not going to get well."*
*   *"It is a sin to want to die."*
*   *"He always wanted to paint a masterpiece."*

## ❓ Critical Analysis (Exam POV)
*   **Significance of the Title:** "The Last Leaf" refers to the painted leaf that became the symbol of hope and Behrman's masterpiece.
*   **Character of Behrman:** A rough exterior but a heart of gold. He gave his life for a young girl.

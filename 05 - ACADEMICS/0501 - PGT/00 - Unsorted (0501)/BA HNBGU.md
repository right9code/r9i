## BA HNBGU Syllabus

**Authors and Works (Syllabus Content):**

1.  **Vinod Sood:** *Individual and Society* (Chapters II, IV, XI, XII, XXVIII)
    *   Premchandra: *Deliverance* (Story within *Individual and Society*)
    *   Ismat Chughtai: *Kallu* (Story within *Individual and Society*)
    *   W.B. Yeats: *A Prayer for my Daughter* (Poem within *Individual and Society*)
    *   Eunice De Souza: *Marriage are Made* (Story within *Individual and Society*)
    *   Nadine Gordimer: *Jump* (Story within *Individual and Society*)
2.  **Selections in English poetry** (H.N.B.G. University publication):
    *   John Milton: *On His Blindness* (Poem)
    *   Thomas Grey: *Elegy Written in a Country Churchyard* (Poem)
    *   William Wordsworth: *The Solitary Reaper* (Poem)
    *   John Keats: *Ode to Nightingale* (Poem)
    *   Robert Browning: *Prospice* (Poem)
3.  **Sukrita Paul:** *Selections from Cultural Diversity, Linguistic Plurality and Literary Traditions in India* (Specifically Tribal Verse Unit-4, Writing in English Unit-6)
4.  **William Shakespeare:** *The Merchant of Venice* (Play)
5.  **Charles Dickens:** *Oliver Twist* (Novel)
6.  **Saadant Hussan Manto:** *Toba Tek Singh* (Short Story)
7.  **Vijay Tendulkar:** *Silence, The Court is in Session* (Play)
8.  **A. P. J. Abdul Kalam:** *Wings of Fire* (Book - GE-I option)
9.  **Will Durant:** *The Origin of Science* (Book - GE-I option)
10. **Govind Chatak** (translated by Arun Pant): *The Folk Tales of Uttarakhand* (Book - GE-II option)
11. **Indira Goswami:** *The Shadow of Kamakhya* (Book - GE-II option)
12. **Mahasweta Devi:** *Mother of 1084* (Book - GE-II option)

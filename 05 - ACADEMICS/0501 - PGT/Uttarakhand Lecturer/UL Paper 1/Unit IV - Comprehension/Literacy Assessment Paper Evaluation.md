### **How to use this for your Weekly Lessons:**

1. **Identify the "Red Zones":** Any score in the lower bracket of a set is a **Red Zone**. This becomes your Priority 1 for the first 3 weeks.
    
2. **Identify the "Strengths":** Any score in the upper bracket is a **Green Zone**. Use these to build her confidence at the start of every lesson.
    
3. **The Weekly Goal:** Pick **one** Red Zone from Hindi, **one** from English, and **one** from Math to work on each Sunday.

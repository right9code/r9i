# 4. Restoration and 18th Century (1660-1798)

## Historical and Cultural Context

-   **1660**: Restoration of monarchy under Charles II
-   **1665-1666**: Great Plague and Great Fire of London
-   **1685**: James II becomes king
-   **1688**: Glorious Revolution; William and Mary take throne
-   **1702-1714**: Reign of Queen Anne
-   **1707**: Act of Union creates Great Britain
-   **1714**: Beginning of Hanoverian dynasty
-   **1721-1742**: Robert Walpole as first "Prime Minister"
-   **1740-1748**: War of Austrian Succession
-   **1756-1763**: Seven Years' War
-   **1760-1820**: Reign of George III
-   **1773**: Boston Tea Party
-   **1776**: American Declaration of Independence
-   **1789**: French Revolution begins
-   **1793**: France declares war on Britain

Here's a 4-week sit-up progression plan to help you go from 10 to 100 sit-ups.
Week 1: Build Endurance
Day 1: 3 sets of 10
Day 2: 4 sets of 10
Day 3: 3 sets of 12
Day 4: Rest
Day 5: 4 sets of 12
Day 6: 3 sets of 15
Day 7: Rest
Week 2: Increase Volume
Day 8: 3 sets of 15
Day 9: 4 sets of 15
Day 10: 3 sets of 18
Day 11: Rest
Day 12: 4 sets of 18
Day 13: 3 sets of 20
Day 14: Rest
Week 3: Push to Higher Reps
Day 15: 3 sets of 20
Day 16: 4 sets of 20
Day 17: 3 sets of 25
Day 18: Rest
Day 19: 4 sets of 25
Day 20: 3 sets of 30
Day 21: Rest
Week 4: Reach 100 Sit-ups
Day 22: 3 sets of 30
Day 23: 4 sets of 30
Day 24: 3 sets of 35
Day 25: Rest
Day 26: 2 sets of 50
Day 27: 1 set of 70 + 1 set of 30
Day 28: 1 set of 100!
Tips for Success
✔ Maintain proper form to avoid injury. ✔ If you feel too sore, take an extra rest day. ✔ Engage your core and breathe properly. ✔ You can adjust the plan if needed—listen to your body!
